Our Story

Born from a love of beautiful things

Mzuri Coffee was born in Randburg with one conviction: that exceptional coffee should feel like belonging. We source ethically, brew with intention, and believe every person who walks through our door deserves to feel at home.

"Mzuri" — the Swahili word for beautiful — is our promise to you. Beautiful coffee. Beautiful community. Beautiful moments, every single day.

Community first
We are a gathering point for Randburg's creative, curious and connected. Our door is always open.
Ethically sourced
Every bean tells a story. We trace ours from farm to cup, ensuring farmers are paid fairly.
Craft over convenience
We take the time to get it right. Every extraction, every pour, every single time.
